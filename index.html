Asanas

  • Asanas, also known as yoga poses, are physical postures that are practiced in the ancient art of yoga. They are a fundamental aspect of yoga and play a vital role in cultivating physical strength, flexibility, balance, and mindfulness.

Pranayama

  • Pranayama is a Sanskrit term that refers to the practice of breath control in yoga. It is derived from two words: "Prana" meaning life force or vital energy, and "Ayama" meaning extension or expansion. Pranayama techniques involve regulating and manipulating the breath to achieve various physical, mental, and spiritual benefits.

Meditation

  • Meditation is a practice that involves training the mind to focus and redirect thoughts, leading to a state of clarity, calmness, and inner peace. It is an ancient practice found in various cultures and spiritual traditions, including yoga, Buddhism, and mindfulness-based approaches.
